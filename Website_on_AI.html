What is Artificial Intelligence?

Machine Learning

Algorithms that learn from data to make predictions and decisions without explicit programming.

Deep Learning

Neural networks with multiple layers that mimic the human brain for complex pattern recognition.

Generative AI

AI systems that create new content, from text and images to music and code.

The History of AI

Ancient Origins

Concepts of artificial beings appear in ancient Greek mythology and mechanical automata of the 18th century.

1950s: Birth of AI

Alan Turing proposes the Turing Test. The Dartmouth Conference establishes AI as a field of study.

1960s-1970s: Early AI

Development of expert systems, natural language processing, and the first AI programs.

1980s-1990s: Neural Networks

Revival of connectionism and development of convolutional neural networks.

2000s: Big Data Era

Increased computational power enables machine learning at scale.

2010s-Present: AI Revolution

Deep learning breakthroughs, generative AI, and widespread adoption across industries.
